Purpose

The purpose of this site manifold. First off, it provides a clear and concise description of the currriculum for students, parents, and faculty at the school where I teach. Second, it provides a place for my creations to be easily accessible to students. Third, it shares resources with those in the community of teachers who are looking for education materials or insight on how to teach some of this curriculum.
