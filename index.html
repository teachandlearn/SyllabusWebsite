Welcome

Welcome to my website! This website serves as an introduction to me, to the classes I teach, and to instructional materials that I have created.

About Me

My name is Mr. Hillegonds, and I am an educator who believes in being interested in everything. In my teaching, my goal is to allow students to learn as much as they can about the world, eachother, and themselves. As they dig into learning as much as they can about as many different subjects as they can, they develop the skills and techniques necessary for lifelong learning, collaborative creating, critical thinking, and problem solving.

Credentials

My path as an educator has varied over the years. I have been a reading, math, computer science, and science teacher. I have also led a science club and a coding club at my school for several years. My credentials are as follows:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Secondary Biology Education from Purdue University
  • Indiana Licenses In: Biology, Computer Science, Middle School Math
  • A Plethora of Training in Programming
  • Seven Years of Educating
